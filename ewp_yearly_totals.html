Yearly Precipitation Patterns

Yearly precipitation in England & Wales can vary greatly from year to year. Some years are marked by persistent wet weather and frequent storms, while others experience extended dry spells or droughts. The yearly total reflects the combined effects of seasonal rainfall patterns, extreme events, and long-term climate trends.

How to Use This Visualization

Interactive Features: Click on any data point to see detailed information about that year's total precipitation. The visualization will show annotations with the exact year and precipitation value.

Trend Analysis: Look for long-term trends in annual rainfall. Are years getting wetter or drier overall? The data allows for comprehensive analysis of yearly rainfall patterns and extremes.

Extremes: The wettest and driest years are highlighted. Compare these to see the range of variability in annual precipitation.

About WeatherArchives

WeatherArchives provides comprehensive weather data analysis and visualization tools, starting with the Central England Temperature (CET) record - one of the world's longest continuous climate datasets.

Our interactive tools help researchers, students, and weather enthusiasts explore climate patterns, identify trends, and understand long-term changes in weather data.

Getting Started

Begin your exploration by clicking on "Temperature" in the navigation menu above, or use the feature cards to dive directly into our temperature analysis tools.

Each visualization offers different insights into climate patterns, from daily extremes to long-term trends spanning over 250 years of data.

Data Sources

Our analysis uses official datasets maintained by meteorological organizations, including the Central England Temperature record from the UK Met Office.

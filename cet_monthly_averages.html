Monthly Temperature Analysis

This visualization shows temperature trends for the selected month over the entire dataset. Use the dropdown above to select different months and explore how temperatures have changed over time for each month of the year.

Interactive Features: Click on any data point to see detailed information about that year's temperature. The visualization will show annotations with the exact year and temperature value.

Trend Analysis: Look for patterns in how the selected month has changed over time. Some months may show more dramatic warming or cooling trends than others.
